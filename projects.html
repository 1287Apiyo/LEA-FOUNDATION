PROJECT HIGHLIGHTS

Mukuru Slums Development Project (MSDP)

LEA Foundation partners with the Mukuru Slums Development Project to offer coding and technology exposure, empowering youth with essential skills for future opportunities.

Coding sessions

Our first cohort completed an introductory web development course covering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, equipping them with essential skills to create basic web applications and boosting their confidence in the tech field.

Additional Visuals from This Experience

Examinations

Students took a rigorous exam to evaluate their skills and understanding, identifying strengths and areas for improvement. This assessment, which included practical and theoretical components, ensures they are prepared for real-world tech challenges.

Additional Visuals from This Experience

Hackathon

A thrilling hackathon was held to promote teamwork and apply skills learned. Participants formed teams to tackle real-world problems, showcasing their creativity and receiving mentorship from industry professionals.

RAI Alliance Partnership

The RAI Alliance project enhances our coding initiative by integrating AI technologies into our curriculum. Through workshops and resources, we empower learners with essential skills, fostering awareness of AI's impact on future careers.

AI Exposure

We partner with RAI Alliance to empower local communities through tech services, offering digital literacy training and access to resources. This initiative enhances tech skills and fosters economic development.
