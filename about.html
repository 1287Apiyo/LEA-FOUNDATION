Founders

LEA Foundation was founded by a group of passionate individuals dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Mukuru Slums. Their collective vision and commitment to providing access to technology education have transformed the lives of countless children. Through their leadership and guidance, the foundation continues to flourish, bringing hope and opportunities to those who need it most.

Foundation Day

Founded in May 2024, LEA Foundation was born out of a deep desire to make a tangible difference in the lives of underprivileged children in Mukuru Slums. Our mission is to offer quality technology education that paves the way for a brighter future.

Background Story

The LEA Foundation emerged as a beacon of hope in response to the glaring disparity in access to technology and education in Mukuru Slums. Our founders witnessed firsthand the struggles faced by children in their pursuit of learning, particularly in the ever-evolving tech landscape. With a vision to bridge this digital divide, LEA Foundation was established to empower these children with the necessary skills to navigate and thrive in a technology-driven world. We began our journey by organizing coding workshops and training sessions, aimed at providing hands-on experience and fostering creativity. Our foundation is not just about imparting knowledge; it's about nurturing a community where every child can envision a future filled with possibilities and opportunities.

Purpose

Our core purpose is to empower learners with essential coding skills and practical knowledge through immersive projects and hands-on training. We strive to make technology education engaging and accessible, nurturing the tech innovators of tomorrow.

Drive

Our drive stems from an unwavering commitment to excellence in technology education. Our dedicated trainers and volunteers work tirelessly to provide each learner with the support and resources needed to succeed and excel in their tech journey.

Achievements

Since our inception, LEA Foundation has achieved significant milestones, including the successful completion of numerous coding workshops, the launch of innovative projects by our students, and recognition from local and international tech communities.

Community Impact

Our foundation's impact extends beyond the classroom. By providing technology education, we empower entire communities, fostering a culture of innovation and opportunity. The skills acquired by our learners not only enhance their overall development but also contribute to the economic growth of their communities.

Mission


To ignite a passion for coding in students through dynamic and inclusive learning experiences, preparing them for the opportunities and challenges of the digital age.

Vision


A future where all students, regardless of background, have the skills and confidence to thrive in a technology-driven world, contributing to a more inclusive and innovative society.
