Volunteer Qualifications

  • Basic coding skills: Have a basic understanding of coding(HTML, CSS, JS).
  • Commitment: Willingness to commit to the program at least two times a month.
  • Passion for teaching: Genuine interest and enthusiasm in inspiring young learners and promoting technology.
  • Adaptability: Ability to work with a diverse group of students, adjusting teaching styles as needed.
  • Training readiness: Willingness to participate in basic training sessions or curriculum overviews provided by the initiative.
